How It Works

Three simple steps from a box of parts to a glowing connection.

handyman

1. Build It

3D print the enclosure. Solder the LEDs onto the custom PCB, or simply use a WS2812B NeoPixel Ring! Wire up the ESP32 and touch sensor. All files are provided free.

memory

2. Flash It

Use our Setup Wizard to enter your details and flash the firmware directly from your browser — no coding required. Chrome and Edge support one-click flashing.

touch_app

3. Tap It

Place both lamps on Wi-Fi, and you're connected. A single tap sends your color to their lamp — instantly, anywhere on Earth.

Ready to build your Linked Lamp?

We provide the 3D files, the PCB schematics, and the software absolutely free. Use our PCB or your own WS2812B NeoPixel Ring.
