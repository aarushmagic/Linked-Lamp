Build Your Linked Lamp

Follow these three steps to go from a box of parts to a pair of glowing, connected lamps. Each lamp takes less than 30 minutes to build! You'll need to build two lamps — one for you and one for the person you want to stay connected with.

Gather Parts

Choose your hardware (custom PCB or NeoPixel ring), download the 3D-printable files, and gather all the components you'll need.

Assemble

Solder your components, wire everything up, and put it all together inside the 3D‑printed enclosure.

Flash Software

Flash the firmware onto both ESP32s, enter your MQTT broker details, and get your personal Web App URLs.
