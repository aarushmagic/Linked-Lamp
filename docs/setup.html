Build Your Linked Lamp

Follow these three steps to go from a box of parts to a pair of glowing, connected lamps. Each lamp takes about an hour to build. You'll need to build two lamps — one for you and one for the person you want to stay connected with.

info

Any Linked Lamp can connect to any other Linked Lamp, whether it's built with NeoPixels or the custom PCB. You can mix and match!
