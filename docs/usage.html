How to Use Your Lamp

You've built and flashed both lamps — here's how to get them running and how to use them day-to-day.

play_circle First-Time Setup

After flashing the firmware, follow these steps to get each lamp online:

  1. Plug in the lamp using a USB-C cable connected to any USB power adapter (5V). The lamp will power on automatically.
  2. Connect to Wi-Fi. On first boot, the lamp creates a Wi-Fi hotspot called "Linked Lamp Setup". Connect to it from your phone.
  3. Choose your network. A configuration page will open automatically. Select your home Wi-Fi network, enter the password, and tap Save. The lamp will restart and connect to your network. A light will blink twice to indicate it is connected to WiFI.
    Tip: The ESP32 only supports 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi. Make sure you're selecting the 2.4 GHz band if your router has separate network names.
  4. Open your Web App. Open your personalized URL in a browser and bookmark it. You were given these URLs at the end of the software setup. If you received this lamp from someone, ask them for your URL.
    NFC Tip: If there is an NFC tag sticker inside your diffuser, tap your phone on the lamp to open the app directly.
  5. Tap the lamp! Once both lamps are connected and both web apps are open, try a tap — the other lamp should light up.

touch_app Touch Gestures

All gestures use the TTP223 touch sensor on the base of the lamp:

Action What happens
Single Tap Sends your default color to the other lamp
Double Tap Turns off your lamp (if it's currently on)
Hold (1.5s+) Cycles through colors — lift your finger to pick one and tap again to send it
Triple Tap Resets Wi-Fi settings (only works when the light on the lamp is off)

phone_iphone The Web App

Your personalized Web App URL (the www.linkedlamp.com/my/ link) is your remote control. Open it in any browser — no app installation needed.

From the web app you can:

  • Send a color — pick any color and tap send
  • Use presets — quick-send "I Love You", "I Miss You", or custom saved messages
  • Change your default color — the color sent when you physically tap the lamp
  • Set the lamp-on duration — how long your lamp stays lit (1–30 minutes)
  • Adjust brightness — set maximum brightness level for your lamp
  • Schedule nighttime mode — reduce brightness or turn the lamp off during quiet hours
  • Check for firmware updates — download and apply updates wirelessly

Your credentials are saved locally after the first visit, so you can bookmark the URL and open it directly next time.

Important: The web app works through your MQTT broker. Both lamps must be flashed together for them to communicate.

system_update Firmware Updates

Your lamp automatically checks for updates every 7 days. You can also manually check by opening the web app, going to Settings, and tapping "Check for Update".

Updates are downloaded over Wi-Fi and applied automatically. The lamp reboots after installing. If a new firmware version crashes, the lamp automatically rolls back to the previous working version.

help_outline Quick Troubleshooting

Problem Solution
Lamp won't connect to Wi-Fi Triple-tap (while powered but lights are off) to reset Wi-Fi, then reconnect via the "Linked Lamp Setup" hotspot. Make sure you're using a 2.4 GHz network.
Other lamp doesn't light up Make sure both lamps are on and connected to Wi-Fi. Open both web apps to verify connectivity.
Web app isn't loading Check that the URL contains your correct broker details. Try opening in Chrome or Edge for best compatibility. If possible, tap your phone on the NFC tag sticker inside your diffuser to open the app directly.
